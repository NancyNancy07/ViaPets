History

VIAPets was established as a combined pet shop and kennel, designed to offer both pet sales and high-quality care services under one roof. Initially, the business focused on pet sales, providing a variety of animals including dogs, cats, birds, fish, and small mammals. As VIAPets grew, it recognized a demand for reliable pet care during holidays, business trips, and other occasions when owners could not be with their pets. In response, the business expanded to include kennel services, offering a safe and comfortable boarding option that meets the diverse needs of pet owners.

This unique model allows VIAPets to serve as a one-stop destination for both purchasing and boarding pets, providing peace of mind that all animals receive dedicated care and attention. Over time, VIAPets has built a strong reputation for responsible animal handling and outstanding customer service. The shop and kennel operate with high standards of care, ensuring that each animal is treated with compassion and respect, while knowledgeable staff offer guidance to customers about their new companions.

Through its commitment to ethical practices, exceptional care, and community trust, VIAPets has grown to become a valued name in the pet industry, providing pet owners with both companionship and reliable support in caring for their animals.

Vision

VIAPets envisions a future where every animal, regardless of size, breed, or background, is given the utmost care and respect as a "Very Important Animal" (VIA). Our goal is to set the highest standard in pet care and sales by creating a safe, nurturing, and supportive environment for all animals. Whether a pet is temporarily staying in our kennel or awaiting adoption by a new family, we prioritize their well-being, comfort, and happiness.

At VIAPets, we believe that our responsibility extends beyond merely selling pets or providing temporary care. We strive to foster a compassionate community centered around animal welfare and customer satisfaction, where pet owners can feel confident that their beloved companions are in the best possible hands. Our commitment to animal welfare is demonstrated through rigorous care protocols, dedicated and trained staff, and ethical practices that emphasize respect for each animal’s unique needs.

VIAPets is dedicated to continuously enhancing our services to meet the evolving needs of our customers and their pets. We invest in ongoing staff training, facility improvements, and customer feedback to ensure our standards remain exceptional. Furthermore, we aim to contribute positively to society by advocating for responsible pet ownership, supporting local animal welfare initiatives, and educating our community on the importance of compassion towards animals. In doing so, VIAPets aspires to be more than just a pet shop and kennel—we aim to be a trusted partner in the lives of pet owners and a model for ethical practices in the pet industry.
